What does Opiniate do?
Opiniate allows you to explore the feelings and opinions of thousand of others in your city (currently only Prague), and identify the places people love, hate or have mixed feelings. Besides providing an overview of the current mood of the city, the dashboard also details the specific comments people have about certain locations. Start by choosing an overall feeling you would like to explore and perhaps even zoom in to an area if you like!

Questions/ Statements
  •   1: This is where I spend my free time
  •   2: I would show this place to a visitor
  •   3: This place is neglected & needs renovation
  •   4: I don't feel safe here
  •   5: There is a traffic hazard here
  •   6: There are parking problems
  •   7: I would like more green space here
  •   8: There is often overflowing waste here
